About Stone Pillars

Stone Pillars in Jaisalmer Sandstone

Decorative stone pillars in Jaisalmer sandstone are among our most sought-after products. The stone's warm golden color and excellent carving qualities make it perfect for classical Indian, Rajasthani, Mughal, and modern architectural styles. Each pillar can be a standalone work of art or part of a coordinated architectural composition.

Our pillar range includes single-piece turned columns, stacked pillar sections, ornate carved capitals, plain square and round columns, and fully carved pillars with intricate traditional motifs. Heights range from 60cm decorative pieces to full structural-height pillars for hotels, temples, and large residential projects.

We use both traditional hand-carving and CNC machining depending on the design requirements. Traditional designs with organic motifs (floral, figurative) benefit from hand-carving, while geometric and symmetrical designs achieve superior consistency with CNC. Many pillars combine both techniques.

Pillar FAQ

Common Questions

What heights and diameters are available for stone pillars?
We manufacture pillars from 60cm decorative pieces up to 300cm+ tall columns. Diameters range from 15cm to 60cm+. For larger heights, we use a stacked section system with precision joints. Custom dimensions are available. Please specify your height, diameter, and design requirements when requesting a quote.
Can pillars be made in one piece or are they sectioned?
Both options are available. Single-piece pillars up to approximately 150–180cm are possible depending on design. Taller pillars are made in precisely cut sections (typically 90–120cm each) that stack together with hidden joints. Sectioned pillars are structurally sound and visually indistinguishable from single-piece columns.
What design styles are available for stone pillars?
We offer traditional Indian styles including Nagara, Dravida, and Rajputana column designs; Mughal-inspired styles with geometric and arabesque carving; classical Roman-inspired columns; modern plain round and square columns; and fully custom designs from architectural drawings. Samples and design catalogs are available on request.
