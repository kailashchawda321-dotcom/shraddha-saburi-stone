Heritage Stone Supply

Authentic Stone for Heritage Architecture

Jaisalmer's built heritage — the Golden Fort, ornate havelis, intricately carved stepwells and temples — represents centuries of architectural mastery. All of this heritage was built from Jaisalmer sandstone, the same stone we quarry and process today. When restoration or renovation is needed, authentic Jaisalmer sandstone is the only appropriate choice.

Our heritage stone supply service includes color matching, texture matching, carved element reproduction, and sourcing specific grades of stone that match the character of existing heritage stonework. We have helped restore temples, havelis, heritage hotels, and government-protected monuments across Rajasthan and beyond.

For new heritage-inspired builds — boutique hotels, luxury residences, cultural centers — we supply authentic Jaisalmer sandstone with aged or natural finishes that evoke the timeless character of Rajasthani heritage architecture, creating spaces that feel genuinely rooted in tradition.

Heritage FAQ

Common Questions

Can you match the color and texture of existing heritage stonework?
Yes, stone matching for heritage restoration is one of our specializations. By examining samples or photographs of existing stone, our quarry managers identify the appropriate grade and layer of Jaisalmer sandstone that best matches the color, grain, and texture. While natural variation means a perfect match may not always be possible, we consistently achieve excellent visual matches.
Do you have experience supplying stone for government heritage projects?
Yes, we have supplied Jaisalmer sandstone for government-sanctioned heritage conservation and restoration projects in Rajasthan and other states. We maintain documentation standards required for heritage projects and can supply test certificates and material specifications on request.
What carved elements can you reproduce for heritage restoration?
We can reproduce virtually any carved heritage element including pillar capitals, decorative brackets, carved jali screens, relief panels, cornices, column bases, doorway surrounds, and figurative carvings. Our master shilpis study reference photographs and measurements to recreate authentic pieces. Complex items may require a prototype approval process.
