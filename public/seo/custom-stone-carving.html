Custom Carving Service

Bespoke Stone Carving in Jaisalmer Sandstone

Jaisalmer sandstone is celebrated for its exceptional carvability. Its relatively fine grain and consistent texture allow stone carvers to achieve extraordinary levels of detail — from gossamer-thin jali screens to deeply carved three-dimensional sculptures. At Shraddha Saburi Stone, our team of master shilpis (traditional stone artisans) trained in classical Indian stone carving techniques create bespoke pieces for clients worldwide.

We produce custom carvings for residential, commercial, hospitality, religious, and art contexts. Our service begins with your idea — a photograph, a sketch, an architectural drawing, or a verbal description — and our design team creates a detailed carving plan. For complex pieces, we produce a small prototype for approval before full production.

For pieces requiring geometric precision and perfect symmetry — such as decorative panels, repeat motifs, and architectural elements — we use CNC stone carving technology. For organic forms, figurative work, and pieces requiring artistic interpretation, our master shilpis carve entirely by hand. Many complex pieces use both approaches in combination.

Carving FAQ

Common Questions

How do I start a custom carving order?
Share your requirements with us — a photograph, sketch, description, or CAD file. Our design team will create a detailed proposal including dimensions, design interpretation, technique (hand or CNC), lead time, and price. For complex pieces, we produce a small sample or prototype for your approval before full-scale production. Contact us on WhatsApp +91 9649299121 or call +91 7023912171.
What is the lead time for custom stone carvings?
Lead time depends on complexity, size, and quantity. Simple decorative panels: 15–30 days. Standard figurines and sculptures: 30–60 days. Complex multi-piece commissions or very large pieces: 60–120 days. Rush orders may be possible at a premium. We provide a specific timeline in our quotation after reviewing your requirements.
Can you ship custom carvings internationally?
Yes, we export custom stone carvings worldwide including to UAE, USA, UK, Australia, and Europe. All pieces are individually wrapped in foam, packed in custom wooden crates, and cushioned to prevent any transit damage. We provide complete export documentation. Freight can be arranged on FOB or CIF basis through our freight forwarding partners.
