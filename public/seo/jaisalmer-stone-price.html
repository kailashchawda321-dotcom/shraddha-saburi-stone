Pricing Factors

What Affects Sandstone Price

Several variables determine the final rate for Jaisalmer yellow sandstone. Understanding these helps you plan your procurement accurately.

Stone Grade & Colour Consistency — Premium grade stone with uniform golden hue commands a higher price. Commercial grade with minor colour variation is more economical for large fill areas.
Surface Finish — Natural (rough-sawn) is the lowest cost. Honed and machine-polished finishes involve additional processing, increasing price by 20–40%.
Thickness — Thicker slabs (40 mm+) cost more due to greater raw material usage. Standard 20–25 mm tiles are the most economical option.
Custom Cutting & Sizing — Non-standard sizes, radius cuts, and edge profiling add processing costs. High-volume custom orders reduce the per-piece premium.
Order Quantity — Volume discounts apply from 500 sqft onwards for standard products. Container-load orders (1,500+ sqft) receive the best factory rates.
Design Complexity (Jali & Carvings) — Intricate jali patterns and sculptural carvings are priced by design complexity, artisan hours, and stone weight — not purely by area.

Frequently Asked

Pricing Questions Answered

What is the minimum order quantity for Jaisalmer sandstone?

The minimum order quantity for standard slabs and tiles is typically 100 square feet for domestic orders. For export orders, a full 20-foot container (approximately 1,500–2,000 sqft depending on thickness) is the standard MOQ, though smaller LCL shipments can be arranged for sample or trial orders.

What is the price of Jaisalmer sandstone per square foot?

Jaisalmer sandstone prices range from ₹35 to ₹250 per square foot depending on the product type, finish, thickness, and grade. Standard flooring tiles start around ₹35–65/sqft, honed slabs are ₹55–90/sqft, jali ranges ₹80–180/sqft based on design complexity, and carved decorative elements start from ₹150/sqft. Contact us for an exact quote based on your specifications.

What are the export prices for Jaisalmer sandstone in USD or AED?

Export pricing on FOB Mundra port basis: standard sandstone slabs and tiles are typically USD 3.50–8.00 per square foot (AED 12–29/sqft) depending on thickness and finish. Jali panels and carved elements are priced individually based on design. Prices include factory packaging and loading. Ocean freight, insurance, and destination port charges are additional.

Can custom sizes and thicknesses be priced separately?

Yes. Custom cutting, sizing, and thickness are available with additional processing charges. Customisation costs depend on the complexity of the cut, the finish required, and the quantity ordered. Larger quantities reduce the per-unit custom processing cost significantly.
